F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is an NFT?

    NFTs ("Non-Fungible Tokens") are one-of-a-kind tokens that represent a unique good or asset, like digital art. Our NFTs are created on the Ethereum blockchain and the artwork of each NFT is hosted on decentralised file storage, IPFS.

  • What can I do with this NFT?

    Each NFT serves as a digital proof that you donated to support Ukraine in their darkest hours. Once you receive an NFT, it is yours for good. You may use it for any number of things.

    • Set them as profile pictures. Twitter supports NFTs as verified profile pictures
    • Give it to someone else to show that you donated on their behalf
    • Resell on Opensea. See the FAQ on this below

  • How much does the team make from this project?

    None, 100% of donations go to Ukraine. If users resell their NFTs on Opensea we have a 10% royalty (maximum allowed by Opensea) that will again go directly to the official Ukraine wallet.

  • Why 24,891 NFTs?

    Ukraine declared independence on 24 August 1991.

  • Why each NFT costs 0.024081991 ETH?

    Ukraine declared independence on 24 August 1991. And we want to keep the amount accessible for smaller donations too.

  • Can I list them on Opensea?

    We don't like the idea of people speculating on the price of these NFTs. We ask that you don't misuse them.

    If you decide to sell these NFTs on Opensea, we have a 10% royalty (maximum allowed by Opensea) that will again go directly to the official Ukraine wallet.

  • Can I verify the code?

    Well of course! As they say in the crypto space...Don't Trust, Verify!

    Our smart contract is open sourced and you can verify it here.

  • I connected with Email Id. Where's my Wallet?

    Connect with Email Id is powered by Torus. You can checkout your wallet here.

  • Why do I only see the Ukraine flag for the NFT?

    To prevent people from gaming the system, by donating only when a "good" NFT is ahead, we hide all our NFTs and reveal them in batches. This way, everyone gets a random artwork as NFT and no donation is delayed in anticipation. This is a standard process in the NFT world - mint and reveal.

    Each batch is released when a certain donations target is reached and everyone in that batch will see the final artwork associated with the NFT they received.

    • NFT #4200 minted - Batch 0 is revealed (1-4199 will have the artwork)
    • NFT #8400 minted - Batch 1 is revealed (1-8399 will have the artwork)
    • NFT #12600 minted - Batch 2 is revealed (1-12599 will have the artwork)
    • NFT #16800 minted - Batch 3 is revealed (1-4199 will have the artwork)
    • NFT #21000 minted - Batch 4 is revealed (1-4199 will have the artwork)
    • NFT #24891 minted - Batch 5 is revealed (all will have the artwork)

  • Are there other ways to donate to Ukraine?

    Yes! There are many ways to donate to Ukraine and Charitable organisations helping the people displaced by this crisis.

    • The Ukraine Government bank details for donations in fiat (USD, EURO, GBP, INR etc).
    • Official crypto wallets setup by the Ukraine Government. All donations made through Give UA Org will automatically go here.
